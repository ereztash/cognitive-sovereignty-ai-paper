The quiet cost of frictionless AI

Offloading effort to AI boosts output now — but uncalibrated offloading is linked to weaker critical thinking and memory. The evidence is mounting:

72%

of knowledge workers report less mental effort on thinking tasks when using generative AI.

Lee et al., Microsoft & CMU, CHI 2025
