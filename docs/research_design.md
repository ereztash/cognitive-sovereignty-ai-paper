# Research Design

## Primary research question

When does AI-mediated cognitive offloading strengthen cognition, and when does it weaken cognitive sovereignty?

## Proposed empirical design

Three-arm study:

1. No AI
2. Uncalibrated AI
3. Fortified AI

## Task

Participants complete a complex writing, synthesis, or reasoning task.

## Outcome measures

- Immediate task quality
- Delayed recall
- Independent reconstruction
- AI-error detection
- Confidence calibration
- User ownership of reasoning
- Cognitive sovereignty score

## Fortified AI intervention

The AI interface requires:

- pre-answer prediction
- confidence rating
- user hypothesis
- AI output review
- counterargument generation
- independent reconstruction after delay

## Expected result

Uncalibrated AI should improve immediate fluency but reduce retention and sovereignty. Fortified AI should produce the best joint outcome.
